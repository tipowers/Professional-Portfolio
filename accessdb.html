I Stumbled Onto Creating a Database

My first experience with development!

Click Here For Github Code

During my Human Resource Management Internship at The Damien Center I overhead a conversation about needing an e-form and database for their clients. I had recently taken a Computers in Business course which introduced me to Microsoft Access and I naively agreed to take on the project. After all it was a simple one-page form right?

Almost immediately I realized I was in over my head, but I saw it as a challenge to overcome. I spent all of my free time working on the project. I reached out to my Access professor on three separate occasions and she gladly pointed me in the right direction.

In the end, I finished the project on time and had a blast learning to use Microsoft Access in the process. My professor was thoroughly impressed with what I was able to accomplish with no experience. She told me projects such as this are done in the real-world by teams of mostly seasoned developers. She told me that I should be proud. I am!
