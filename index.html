Get To Know Me

Introducing Tim Powers ... 'Web Developer Extraordinaire'

So maybe the 'extraordinaire' part is a bit of a stretch, or is it? Either way, I'm just a fun-loving, hard-working, dedicated Web Developer based out of Noblesville, Indiana. I love development so much I find hours go by like minutes. Web Development allows me to put to the test my attention-to-detail and determination to overcome obstacles. I love a challenge!

In May of 2020 I earned an Associates in Software Development after years of telling myself I was too old to get into development. I had already started and succeeded at one career and I was hesitant to start over, but I loved computers and wanted to get into development. It took my college advisor telling me he didn't start his development career until he was 47 years old. That was all the motivation I needed. Now here I am, doing what I love every day!
